# Product Overview

## Moqui Framework Application

This is a comprehensive Moqui Framework application that demonstrates enterprise-grade business functionality across multiple domains. Moqui is a Java-based framework for building business applications with XML-driven configuration and Groovy scripting.

## Core Business Domains

- **E-Commerce (PopCommerce)** - Complete order management, product catalog, pricing, and inventory
- **Project Management (HiveMind)** - Task management, project collaboration, wikis, and time tracking  
- **Manufacturing (MarbleERP)** - Production planning, work orders, and bill of materials management
- **Master Data Management (mantle-udm)** - Universal data model for parties, organizations, and core entities
- **Reusable Services (mantle-usl)** - Common business logic patterns and service implementations
- **UI Framework (SimpleScreens)** - Dashboards, screens, forms, and reporting components

## Key Characteristics

- **Component-Based Architecture** - Modular design with independent business components
- **XML Configuration** - Entity definitions, services, and screens defined in XML
- **Multi-Database Support** - PostgreSQL (recommended), MySQL, H2, Derby
- **Search Integration** - OpenSearch/Elasticsearch for full-text search and analytics
- **Docker Ready** - Complete containerization with Docker Compose orchestration
- **Enterprise Features** - Multi-tenancy, security, workflow, reporting, and integration capabilities

## Target Use Cases

- Enterprise resource planning (ERP) systems
- E-commerce platforms with complex business rules
- Project management and collaboration tools
- Manufacturing and supply chain management
- Custom business applications requiring rapid development
